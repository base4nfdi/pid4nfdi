            Persistent identifiers (PIDs) are provided by different organizations, so-called PID providers. While often one provider focuses on one type of PID, a provider may offer several different types of PIDs. Vice versa, one type of PID can be offered by more than one provider — as in the case of DOIs — or be exclusive to one particular provider — as in the case of ORCID iDs. The following table gives an overview of PID providers and the PIDs they offer. Please note that this list is not comprehensive and is a work in progress. We welcome any [suggestions](https://pid.services.base4nfdi.de/about/contact) for additional PIDs or providers.

            Not sure which PID fits your use case? Try our **[PID Selection Tool](https://pid.services.base4nfdi.de/pidtool/)** or browse the **[PID4NFDI Cookbook](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)** for practical guidance on individual PID types.

            | **Logo** | **Provider** | **PID type** | **For what entities** | **How to join / register** | **Cookbook entry** |
            |---|---|---|---|---|---|
            | ![](ark.png) | [ARK Alliance](https://arks.org/) | ARK (Archival Resource Key) | Datasets, text publications, images, software, and other digital objects | Free NAAN registration | [ARK](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/ark.html) |
            | ![](crossref.png) | [Crossref](https://www.crossref.org/) | DOI | Text publications, datasets, software, reports, conference papers | Institutional membership (fee) | [DOI](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/doi.html) |
            | ![](datacite.png) | [DataCite](https://datacite.org/) | DOI | Datasets, software, instruments, text publications, and other research outputs | Institutional membership (fee) | [DOI](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/doi.html) |
            | ![](urn.png) | [DNB (Deutsche Nationalbibliothek)](https://www.dnb.de/DE/Professionell/Services/URN-Service/urn-service_node.html) | URN (Uniform Resource Name) | Text publications, datasets, websites | Free, institutional namespace request | — |
            | ![](epic.png) | [ePIC (Persistent Identifier Consortium for eResearch)](https://www.pidconsortium.net/) | ePIC Handle | Datasets, instruments, digital objects, software | Institutional membership (fee) | [ePIC](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/epic.html) |
            | ![](factgrid.png) | [FactGrid](https://database.factgrid.de/) | FactGrid QID | Persons, organisations, historical objects, events — primarily for humanities research | Free account registration | [FactGrid](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/factgrid.html) |
            | ![](gnd.png) | [GND (Gemeinsame Normdatei)](https://www.dnb.de/gnd) | GND-ID | Persons, organisations, places, subjects, works | Free, requires GND network membership or cooperation agreement | [GND](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/gnd.html) |

            | ![](pida.png) | [PIDA (Helmholtz Metadata Collaboration)](https://purls.helmholtz-metadaten.de/) | PURL (Persistent URL) | Semantic artifacts (ontologies, terminologies, schemas) | Free registration | [PIDA](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/pida.html) |
            | ![](pidinst.png) | [PIDINST / RDA (via DataCite or ePIC)](https://www.rd-alliance.org/groups/persistent-identification-instruments-wg) | DOI or ePIC Handle | Instruments | Institutional membership (fee, via DataCite or ePIC) | [PID for Instruments](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/pidinst.html) |
            | ![](raid.png) | [RAiD / ARDC](https://www.raid.org/) | RAiD | Research projects and activities | Free service; institutional sign-up via ARDC or regional Registration Agency | [RAiD](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/raid.html) |
            | ![](ror.png) | [ROR (Research Organization Registry)](https://ror.org/) | ROR ID | Organisations (research institutions) | Free, editorial review process | [ROR](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/ror.html) |
            | ![](swhid.png) | [Software Heritage](https://www.softwareheritage.org/) | SWHID | Software, source code | Free, no registration required for archiving | — |
            | ![](wikidata.png) | [Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/) | Wikidata QID | Persons, organisations, concepts, places, and many other entity types | Open, no registration required | [Wikidata](https://pid4nfdi-training.readthedocs.io/en/latest/wikidata.html) |

            _This list is sorted alphabetically by provider name. It is a work in progress — entries marked "—" do not yet have a Cookbook article. [Suggestions and corrections are welcome.](https://pid.services.base4nfdi.de/about/contact)_
